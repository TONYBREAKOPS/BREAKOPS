HOW MANDATEOPS WORKS

ELIGIBILITY

Only employees whose shift ends at the block start time are shown. Absent employees, those with existing OT in that block, or anyone who would exceed 16 hours are excluded.

STEPS

1. Import OT first. Upload a Teams export (.xlsx/.csv) to load each employee's OT from the last 7 days. Resets on page reload.

2. Pick a date, shift, and the 4-hour block you are mandating for, then click Generate.

3. Click Mandate if the employee accepts, or Refuse if they decline. All actions are logged.

4. Refused employees get a Print Refusal button — prints a formal refusal form for them to sign.

RANKING ORDER

1. Shortest work day  2. Least OT (7 days)  3. Highest seniority

OT hours can be manually adjusted. The list re-ranks automatically.

CROSS-QUALIFICATION

CT positions: check CT, then DP, then PIC.

DP positions: check DP, then PIC.

PIC positions: PIC only.
